About us

Project Management system

This is a project that manages student projects. They register then choose a project of their choice, in one of the areas of specialization.
Each student is assigned to a supervisor who guides them on what to do.
The system allows the student to register their progress. This project has a span of two semesters.
Each student is expected to have a unique project.
